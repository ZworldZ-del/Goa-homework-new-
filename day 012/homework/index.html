WHY DOG AND CATS SHOULD BE TOGETHER

While cats and dogs are often depicted as natural rivals, they can, in fact, coexist peacefully and even form strong bonds when introduced and socialized properly. Having both in a household can offer companionship for each other, enrich their lives, and provide owners with additional benefits.
